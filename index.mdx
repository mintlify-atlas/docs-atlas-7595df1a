NextBus SG is a free Flutter app for iOS and Android that shows you live bus arrival times for stops near you, lets you save favorite buses, and helps you search the full Singapore bus directory. It pulls live data from the [LTA DataMall](https://datamall.lta.gov.sg/) API so timings are always up to date.

## What makes NextBus SG different

Most bus apps show you everything at once — maps, full route lists, every stop in the city. NextBus SG takes the opposite approach: show you only what you're actually going to take, right now.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Context-aware favorites" icon="location-dot">
    When you open the app near a saved stop, your favorite bus timings appear at the top automatically — no searching required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No map clutter" icon="eye-slash">
    Once you know where your stop is, a map wastes screen space. NextBus SG skips the map and gets straight to timings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MRT integration" icon="train-subway">
    Bus stops that are also MRT interchanges are clearly labelled so you always know your transit options at a glance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Light & dark themes" icon="moon">
    Switch between light and dark mode — or follow your system setting — for comfortable reading any time of day.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
