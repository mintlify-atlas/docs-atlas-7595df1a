---
title: "Themes"
description: "Switch between light, dark, and system themes to match your preference."
---

NextBus SG supports three theme options. Your choice is saved locally on your device using Hive, so it persists across app restarts.

## Available themes

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Light" icon="sun">
    Clean white backgrounds with dark text. The default theme.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dark" icon="moon">
    Dark backgrounds that are easier on the eyes in low-light conditions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="System" icon="display">
    Automatically matches your device's appearance setting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The status bar color updates automatically to match whichever theme is active.

## How to change the theme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the More tab">
    Tap the **More** tab — the third tab on the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Settings section">
    Scroll to the **Settings** section at the top of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a theme">
    Tap your preferred theme option: **Light**, **Dark**, or **System**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Choose **System** to have the app automatically switch between light and dark based on your device's dark mode setting. This is useful if you use scheduled dark mode or auto-brightness.
</Tip>
