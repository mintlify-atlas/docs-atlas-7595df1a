---
title: "Renaming Bus Stops"
description: "Give your saved bus stops custom display names so they're easier to recognize at a glance."
---

You can assign a custom display name to any bus stop you've added to your favorites. Renamed stops appear in italics throughout the app so you can tell them apart from stops using their default names.

Names are stored locally on your device using Hive — no account or internet connection required.

<Note>
  You can only rename bus stops that you've added to your favorites. Add a stop to your favorites first before renaming it.
</Note>

## Method 1: Via the More tab

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the More tab">
    Tap the **More** tab — the third tab on the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Rename favorites">
    Tap **Rename favorites** in the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap a bus stop name">
    A list of your favorited stops appears. Tap any stop name to edit it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a new name">
    Type the new display name and confirm. The stop will now appear with your custom name throughout the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Method 2: Via the search page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Search tab">
    Tap the **Search** tab — the second tab on the bottom navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the bus stop">
    Search for the bus stop you want to rename.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Press the Rename button">
    On the stop's result, press the **Rename** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a new name">
    Type the new display name and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I rename a bus stop?">
    There are two ways:

    1. Add the stop to your favorites, then go to the **More** tab (the third tab on the bottom bar) and tap **Rename favorites**.
    2. Search for the bus stop in the **Search** tab (the second tab), then press the **Rename** button on the result.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add a stop to my favorites?">
    **Slide** any bus service tile to the left to reveal the heart icon, then tap it to add the service to your favorites.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I remove a custom name?">
    Go to **More** → **Rename favorites**, tap the stop, and clear the name field. This resets the stop to its default name.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
