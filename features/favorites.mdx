---
title: "Favorites"
description: "Save specific bus services at stops you use regularly, and let NextBus SG surface them automatically when you're nearby."
---

The favorites feature lets you pin specific bus services at specific stops. When you are close to a saved stop, its buses float to the top of the home screen so you can see their timings immediately without scrolling.

## How to add a favorite

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the bus service">
    On the home screen, locate the bus stop and the service number you want to save. You can also find it through the Search page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Slide the tile to the left">
    Swipe any bus service tile to the left to reveal the favorites action button (a heart icon).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the heart button">
    Tap the heart icon to toggle the service in or out of your favorites. The button turns red when a service is already saved, indicating it will be removed if tapped.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also tap the bus service number (e.g. "65") on a tile to open the service detail page, which shows the full route for that service.
</Tip>

## How to remove a favorite

Slide the same bus service tile to the left again. The heart icon will be filled and red, indicating the service is currently saved. Tap it to remove the favorite.

## Context-aware display

<Tip>
  The most useful aspect of favorites is that they are context-aware. If you saved Bus 14 at Bedok Interchange, opening the app while you are near Bedok Interchange will automatically float Bus 14 to the top of the home screen. You do not need to search or scroll.
</Tip>

Favorites are shown at the top of the home screen when the relevant bus stop is within **750 meters** of your current location. This threshold is intentionally slightly larger than the general "near me" distance to account for situations where you are walking toward the stop.

If none of your saved favorites are nearby, the near-me list moves to the top instead, and a message notes how many favorites are saved but not currently shown.

## Simplified favorites view vs full favorites view

**Simplified favorites view (home screen):** Shows only the favorited bus service numbers for each nearby stop, not all services at that stop. This reduces clutter and focuses the display on exactly the buses you care about.

**Full favorites view:** Tap the "See all" button to open a dedicated page listing every saved favorite regardless of your current location. This view shows all favorited services at every saved stop.

## Custom stop names

You can rename any favorited bus stop to a label that is more meaningful to you (for example, "Home stop" instead of the official stop name).

There are two ways to rename a stop:

- From the **More** page, tap "Rename favorites" and tap any stop name in the list.
- From the **Search** page, find the stop and tap the **Rename** button.

For full instructions, see [Renaming stops](/customization/renaming-stops).
